Hardware Developer

AOMS Technologies

At AOMS, I presided over the mechanical research and development of multiple core products. I focused in the sourcing and design of enclosures to house sensitive electrical and optical sensing components using SolidWorks. In addition to my primary tasks, I was exposed to Python in multiple projects such as creating a 3D model of a concrete obstruction and automating the data acquisition and analysis of fiber optic temperature sensors inside a furnace oven. I also created a fiber optic interrogator simulator using Python and Tkinter to plot and analyze data from simulated fiber optic sensors.

Electrical Engineering Coop

During my time at AXYZ, I was responsible for the developing the process of creating new wire harnesses for the Optimus woodworking router and its offloading conveyor and material lifter. My major term project was the creation of custom parts to improve the function of the offloading conveyor's photoelectric sensor. I also created a testing jig for testing the conformance of router spindles.

Junior Process Engineer
